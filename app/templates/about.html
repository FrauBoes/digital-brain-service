About Us

The Digital Brain was imagined, designed, and built by a multidisciplinary team of four students from the Luxembourg Tech School guided by the LTS Coaches Sara Kaiser and Julia Boes.

Blending science fiction with emerging neurotechnologies, and supported by immersive storytelling and performance design, this project became a bold and experimental learning journey - and an invitation to explore what it means to be human in a hyper-technological world.
