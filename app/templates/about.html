Our Team

Punyabhalan - Gabriel - Juliana - Vasileia

Meet Punyabhalan, Gabriel, Juliana and Vasileia, our skilled team of developers. We are four students from Luxembourg Tech School who collaborated to explore the cutting-edge world of neurotechnology. Together, we developed both the narrative and technological aspects of this Digital Brain experience, examining how brain-computer interfaces are transforming from science fiction into reality. Our project invites visitors to contemplate the profound implications of merging human consciousness with artificial intelligence in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape.
