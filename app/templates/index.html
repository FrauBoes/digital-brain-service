The Story & Tools

Set in 2045, the journey began in a world transformed by brain-computer interfaces. Once experimental, these neural technologies now shape how people work, decide, dream - and even remember. You were invited to explore:

Brain Implant (Prototype)

Once a medical breakthrough, now a transformative leap in human evolution. This prototype highlights the surgical integration of advanced technology, enabling bidirectional communication between brain and machine. Designed for cognitive enhancement and memory expansion, it raises also questions of mental privacy.

Neural Headband

This sleek, non-invasive device opens the door to your mind. Equipped with 1,024 micro-electrodes and sensors, it uses advanced electromagnetic mapping to read brain activity with remarkable precision. The headband is your safe, external entry point into the Digital Brain ecosystem.

Voice Ritual at the Echo Point

In this quiet moment, you were invited to whisper a memory, a feeling, or a name into the Echo Point. Nothing was recorded. As neurotechnology moves toward capturing, storing, and even editing memories, the question arises: What does it mean to keep something truly private, truly human, in a world where even your thoughts might no longer be your own?

And then... you were trapped. A simulation locked your consciousness. Only by proving your humanity could you escape.
